GTA V Intro

GTA Grand Theft Auto V (also known as Grand Theft Auto Five, GTA 5, GTA V, or GTAV[2]) is a video game developed by Rockstar North. It is the fifteenth installment in the Grand Theft Auto series. Grand Theft Auto V is set circa 2013 in the city of Los Santos and its surrounding areas and tells the stories of three protagonists: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips. It takes place about five years after the events of Grand Theft Auto IV, and four years after the events of Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars..
